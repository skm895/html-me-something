Chemistry

The U.S. Army Research Laboratory has found a use for our pee! Researchers were surprised to find that when they combined a nano-particle ,that is based on aluminum, with urine, a great deal of hydrogen was released. The rate of hydrogen being released was much greater than when the experiment was conducted with only water.
